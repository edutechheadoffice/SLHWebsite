Introducing Our Logo "Alive in Christ"

These logos were created by the students of Sekolah Lentera Harapan Tomohon through a meaningful process of reflection on this year's school theme, "Alive in Christ," based on Colossians 2:13–14. As students meditated on God's Word, they expressed their understanding of Christ's redeeming love through visual design.
